About Us

About Seervi International School

School Building Photo Placeholder

Seervi International School, located in Jaitaran, Beawar, Rajasthan, is committed to providing a disciplined, value-driven and academically rigorous environment for every student. Our institution blends modern teaching methodology with strong moral grounding to prepare students for both examinations and life.

With qualified faculty, structured curriculum delivery, and a focus on all-round development, the school has become a trusted name for quality education in the region.

  • ✓Modern classrooms and learning infrastructure
  • ✓Focus on discipline, values and holistic growth
